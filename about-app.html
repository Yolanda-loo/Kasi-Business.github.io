About KasiKapital App

Empowering township entrepreneurs to connect with investors and scale their businesses.

Core User Journey

Business Owner:

Sign up with phone number > Quick quiz on business type/needs > Upload 1-min video pitch + basic docs > AI suggests investor matches > Chat/pitch in-app > Secure deal via escrow > Track growth and share impact.

Investor:

Profile setup (risk appetite, sectors) > Browse curated kasi stories > Video calls or events > Invest via integrated payment (e.g., PayFast) > Get updates on ROI and social impact.

Full cycle: 5-10 mins to profile, matches in 24hrs, deals closed in weeks.

Key Screens

  • Home/Dashboard: Personalized feed of matches, success stories, and tips (e.g., "How to pitch like a pro in Soweto").
  • Profile Builder: Drag-and-drop for business photos, auto-fill revenue from WhatsApp Business integration.
  • Match Hub: Swipe-style cards with investor bios, funding fit scores (e.g., "85% match ‚Äì loves food ventures!").
  • Pitch Studio: Record/edit video, add AR filters for fun (e.g., virtual "kasi market stall").
  • Chat & Events: Secure messaging + calendar for live "Investor Braai Nights" (virtual mixers).
  • Impact Tracker: Dashboard showing jobs created, revenue growth‚Äîshareable badges for social proof.
  • Settings: Language toggle (English, isiZulu, Afrikaans), verification badges.

Monetization Ideas

  • Freemium: Basic matches free; premium ($2/month) for priority visibility and analytics.
  • Success Fee: 1-2% on funded deals (capped low for accessibility).
  • Partnerships: Sponsored tips from banks like Capitec; ads from local suppliers.
  • Investor Subscriptions: $10/month for exclusive kasi deal flow.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Low Digital Literacy: Voice-guided onboarding in local languages; partner with community centers for workshops.
  • Trust Issues: Blockchain-verified docs + endorsements from trusted figures (e.g., local celebs like Cassper Nyovest).
  • Funding Gaps: Micro-investor pooling (e.g., 10 people at R500 each).
  • Data Privacy: GDPR-compliant with simple consent flows.
