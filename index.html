No! Just your revenue history (snaps of bank statements or POS) and a 1-minute video telling us who you are.

Is this a loan or equity?

We offer both. You can choose to pay back a loan with interest, or give a small % of ownership for mentorship and cash.

Once you match with an investor and sign the digital agreement, funds sit in Escrow and are released within 48 hours.
